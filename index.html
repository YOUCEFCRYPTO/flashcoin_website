About Flash Coin

Flash Coin is a stablecoin built on the BEP20 (BSC) network. It is designed to provide fast, secure, and low-cost transactions. Backed by USD to maintain value stability, Flash Coin leverages Binance Smart Chain to deliver top-notch performance for global users. Ideal for daily payments, savings, and cross-border transfers.

Transparency

All transactions are recorded on the blockchain and fully verifiable.

Speed

Fast transaction processing thanks to BSC's infrastructure.

Low Fees

Minimal transaction fees make it ideal for micro and bulk transfers.
